DISTANCE DISCO!!!

Questo meraviglioso pezzo di software ci permetterà di fare una festa di compleanno danzereccia nel pieno rispetto delle norme di distanziamento sociale anti Covid, e sarà una bat-ganzata!

Quindi, inizia a pensare ad un costume, o travestimento, il più eccentrico che tu possa immaginare, e partecipa ai festeggiamenti del mio 30esimo compleanno in distance disco!

A seguire tutti i dettagli su come funziona:

Che cosa faccio per il mio compleanno?

  • Quando? Mar 8 Settembre 2020 alle 21:00 ora italiana (per 2 ore)
  • Dove? https://distancedisco.nl/dd/30-anni-di-arturo

Distance Disco è parte ballo, parte gioco.

Cosa ti serve:

  • Un computer + webcam + una buona connessione ad internet
  • Un vestito divertente
  • Voglia di ballare
  • BYOB (i drink ve li dovete fare da soli :D)

Quando vi collegate alla stanza, vedrete i riquadri di altre persone. Tutti hanno una musica diversa, tranne uno.

La vostra missione è quella di capire chi degli altri partecipante sta ballando la TUA musica.

Quando avete capito chi sta ballando la tua musica, inserito quel numero nella casella in fondo allo schermo per raccogliere punti.

Alcuni suggerimenti:

  • Non saltate le canzoni, perchè a volte l’app si blocca
  • Se il numero di partecipanti è pari, sentirete “musica da ascensore” finchè non arriva qualcuno

    • Maggiori informazioni sulle regole del gioco le trovate quì: https://distancedisco.nl/dd/30-anni-di-arturo#rules

    Quindi vestitevi nel modo più assurdo che potete immaginare e ci vediamo nella distance discooooooooooooooooooo!

Greetings!

Tuesday 8th of September 2020 I turn 30!

If you are in a hurry and/or you don’t feel like reading a bunch of text, you can just skip this introductory section and go straight to the juice.

The juice is in the section What am I doing for my birthday?

If on the other hand you enjoy reading, you may proceed.

I don’t think it’s necessary to go on a rant about the crazy times we are living.... At this point the fact that we are under COVID and the unprecedented times we are living is as clichè as saying the book is better than the movie, the grass is always greener on the other side, ignorance is bliss, etc. etc.

Therefore I will spare you the COVID-centric introduction, because I’m sure you’ve had enough of that. If on the other hand you really feel the need to have some of that, I will leave some white space below so you can insert your favorite COVID monologue:

IN THIS SECTION YOU CAN PRINT AND GLUE YOUR OWN COVID MONOLOGUE.

Use all of your creativity, really put your heart into it and I sure this space will not be enough. For this reason I suggest you print your COVID monologue using a sufficiently small font (I recommend a nice font-size: 6).

Below are some points which can serve as inspiration for your monologue:

  • How being in lockdown was crap
  • The infinite queues at the supermarket
  • How the mask makes your glasses foggy
  • How you almost died of hunger when your favorite restaurant shut down and after washing so many dishes your hands started to get fucked.
  • How, under lockdown, the cops stopped you in the streets asking for your self-certification, but you forgot it at home and you got a big fat fine and notice.

Great, now that we have gotten out of the way the obligatory COVID centric introduction, we can move onto the serious stuff.

I care to specify that I am not a covid-skeptic, actually I think that in these times of crisis, it’s especially important to be socially responsible.

Who knows me, though, knows that I am not a fan of quick and easy solutions that are guided by logics of centralized power or even less of technological-solutionism.

I will not enter into the details of this, but if you are interested we can talk about it in person.

This is to say that when I was thinking of what I should do for my 30th birthday, I found myself in crisis. Already birthdays stress me out by default, because you feel like you must do something or you will run the risk of being judged. Now imagine if the birthday in question is your 30th!

I would have liked to do a surf-trip with some friends, but then...COVID. What if I do a nice house party with music and dance? Then I remembered of COVID. How about a nice lunch with a few intimate friends? Do I need to even say it?

Then Maria came with the right inspiration!

DISTANCE DISCO!!!

This marvelous piece of software will allow us to do a dancing birthday party fully respecting all the anti COVID social distancing best practices and it’s going to be epic!

So, start thinking about your costume or dress, the most eccentric you can imagine and join the celebrations for my 30th birthday in distance disco!

Below are the details on how this all works:

What am I doing for my birthday?

  • When? Tue 8th September 2020 at 18:00 UTC (21:00 Rome) for 2 hours
  • Where? https://distancedisco.nl/dd/30-anni-di-arturo

Distance Disco is part dance party, part game!

You will need:

  • a computer + webcam + stable internet
  • a cool outfit
  • your best dance moves
  • BYO drinks

When you log on, you will be put in a room with your fellow dancers. Everybody has a different music, except one. Your mission is to figure out who is dancing to YOUR music. When you have understood who that is, you will put their “number” into the box on the bottom to score points.

Some tips:

  • Don't skip songs, as it sometimes causes a bug.
  • If there is not an even number of people, you will hear "elevator" music until someone joins.

Learn more about the rules of the game and how it works here: https://distancedisco.nl/dd/30-anni-di-arturo#rules

So dress up in the most crazy way you can imagine and I’ll see you in the distance discoooooooooooooooooooooo!

It’s going to be epic!

~ Art.
